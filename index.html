Onkar Pandit

Hi! I recently graduated with Ph.D. in Computer Science from Univesity of Lille, France. I was fortunate to have Dr. Pascal Denis, Prof. Liva Ralaivola, and Prof. Marc Tommasi as my advisors in my doctoral studies. During this period, I was a part of wonderful Magnet team at INRIA, Lille, France.

My doctoral research is focused on Natural Language Processing (NLP) where effective approach is proposed to improve automatic discourse understanding. Specifically, we integrated contextual information present in the text with external information from knowledge graphs for the tasks of temporal relation classification and bridging resolution.

Prior to joining doctoral program, I worked as Project Scientist with Prof. Utpal Garain at his NLP lab at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, India. Before that, I spent four delightful years at Oracle India Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore, India, as a Software developer.

Hire me! I am looking for research position in NLP, Machine Learning in industry. Please contact me (email: oapandit@gmail.com) if you have an open position.
